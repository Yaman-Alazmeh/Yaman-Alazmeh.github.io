As artificial intelligence continues to evolve, the line between human and 
machine interaction grows increasingly blurred. This project introduces a 
system for constructing personalized human digital twins AI-driven. 
Virtual avatars that emulate a user’s personality, communication style, 
and emotional depth within a shared 3D environment. Through fine
tuning compact language models on social dialogue, MBTI personalities, 
and real user data, the system achieves coherent, emotionally aware 
responses that reflect the essence of the individual. These digital twins 
support both voice and text communication and are embedded in a virtual 
city environment built with Unreal Engine, complete with realistic 
MetaHuman avatars, facial animation, and voice cloning. The system 
integrates memory modules, emotional sentiment tracking, and 
reinforcement-based response selection to offer persistent, believable 
interaction. This project demonstrates the potential of digital twins for 
preserving identity, enhancing social presence, and enabling continuous 
human interaction in both personal and professional contexts. 

<br><br>
**The primary aim of this project:**

is to design and implement a personalized human 
digital twin an intelligent and interactive virtual entity that visually and cognitively 
represents a real person within a 3D environment. This digital twin serves as an 
extension of the individual, capable of communication, self-representation, and 
engagement within a virtual world. The specific objectives of the project include: <br><br>
- Enabling Personalized Communication 
Allow users to interact with digital representations of people they know, simulating 
real-life conversations and enabling continuous communication even when the actual 
person is unavailable. <br><br>
- Creating a Visual and Virtual 3D Presence 
Develop a realistic and expressive 3D avatar for each user that embodies their 
appearance and behaviors within a shared virtual environment, enhancing immersion 
and presence. <br><br>
- Reflecting Real-Life Events and Context 
Design the digital twin to stay informed about the user's life events and context, 
allowing it to respond or interact in a way that mirrors real-time developments and 
personal updates. <br><br>
- Simulating the User’s Thinking and Communication Style 
Build an intelligent system that mimics the user’s cognitive patterns, decision-making 
style, and communication tone—capturing how they think, respond, and express 
themselves in conversation. <br><br>
- Generalizing the Framework for Broader Applications 
Ensure that the virtual representation system is not limited to digital twin use cases, 
but can also be adapted for integration into other domains such as education, virtual 
assistants, memory preservation, storytelling, or social simulation platforms.<br>

<br><br>
**Project Features:**<br>
This project offers a set of advanced and user-centered features designed to enhance 
digital presence, communication, and representation in a virtual 3D world. The system 
allows users to create, interact, and engage with intelligent digital counterparts of real 
people—bridging the gap between physical absence and meaningful presence. 
Key features include: <br><br>
- Personalized Digital Twin Creation 
Users can create a fully customized digital twin that mimics their personality, voice, 
communication style, and way of thinking. This avatar is trained to reflect the user's 
ideas, tone, and responses, offering a highly personalized and lifelike experience.
- Autonomous Communication <br>
The digital twin can independently interact with others on the user's behalf, holding 
conversations, answering questions, and sharing opinions—making it possible for 
users to remain present even when they are unavailable. <br>
- Voice and Chat Interaction <br>
Users can engage with other digital twins through voice or text, enabling meaningful 
exchanges of ideas, life updates, and emotional connections. The system supports 
both synchronous and asynchronous communication. 
- Immersive 3D Virtual World <br>
All interactions take place in a visually rich and immersive 3D city, where users and 
their digital twins can walk, explore, meet, and communicate in dynamic virtual 
environments that simulate real-world social interaction. <br>
- Context-Aware Conversations <br>
Each digital twin can maintain memory and context about its user’s life, allowing it to 
represent not only personality but also current life events, preferences, and 
perspectives. 
- Multi-Purpose Usage <br>
Beyond personal use, digital twins created in this platform can be integrated into other 
projects, including educational simulations, storytelling, mentorship environments, 
and virtual memorials. <br>
- Emotional and Voice Realism <br>
With advanced voice synthesis and expression systems, digital twins can replicate 
emotional tones and facial animations. making conversations feel realistic and 
engaging.<br>
